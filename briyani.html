BIRYANI

Steps to make Biryani:

Prepare the Ingredients:

  • Wash the basmati rice until the water is clear, then soak it for 30 minutes to 2 hours.
  • If you’re adding vegetables, cut them into smaller pieces.

Cooking the Biryani:

  • Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add the cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon to the pan. Sauté for a few seconds, then add onions.
  • After the onions turn translucent, add tomatoes and cashew nuts.
  • Add mint leaves, coriander leaves, and green chilies to the pan.
  • Stir and sauté for a minute or so. Then add the ginger and garlic paste.
  • Stir into the mixture in the pan, then sauté for a couple of minutes.
  • Add garam masala, chili powder, carrots, peas and beans.
  • Sauté for a few more minutes, stirring regularly.

Add Water:

  • Pour in 8 cups of water.
  • After adding the water, add salt to taste.
  • Stir everything together, then bring the mixture to a boil.

Add the Rice:

  • Pour the rice into the boiling water.
  • Add the lemon juice.
  • Cover the pot with a tight lid. Let the rice cook until done.
